Bio

Jordan Orion has always been fascinated by the night sky. With a degree in Astrophysics and a heart full of stories, Jordan embarked on a journey to bring the cosmos closer to Earth through words. As the lead writer for Starry Nights, Jordan specializes in translating complex astronomical phenomena into captivating narratives that resonate with readers of all ages. Whether it's the latest on Martian landscapes or the mysteries of black holes, Jordan's articles invite you on an adventure across the universe.

Fun fact: Jordan spends free nights stargazing in the countryside, always on the lookout for shooting stars to wish upon.

Telescope to Text

Jordan's journey into the world of astronomy began with a childhood fascination with the stars, fueled by countless nights spent under the open sky with a telescope in hand. This early love for stargazing evolved into a dedicated academic pursuit, culminating in a degree in astrophysics. Today, Jordan combines this deep scientific knowledge with a flair for writing, offering readers a front-row seat to the latest discoveries and celestial events.
