2024: A Year of Cosmic Discoveries

Unveiling the Universe: Our 2024 Journey Through Space

Join us at Starry Nights for a year filled with celestial wonder and cosmic discovery. Mark your calendars and prepare for these not-to-be-missed astronomical events. Whether you're an avid stargazer or a curious newcomer to the wonders of the universe, our lineup promises something for everyone.

Supermoon Spectacle

Event Date: March 30, 2024 by Jordan Orion

Don't miss the opportunity to witness the largest and brightest Supermoon of the year. Join our live stream for expert insights, or capture the moment and share it with our community using #SupermoonSpectacle.

Perseid Meteor Shower Viewing Party

Event Date: August 12-13th, 2024

Experience the magic of the Perseid meteor shower with up to 100 meteors per hour. Tune into our virtual viewing party for a night of shooting stars and cosmic wonder.

Details

Lunar Eclipse Watch

Event Date: September 18th, 2024

Witness the beauty of a partial lunar eclipse. We'll guide you through the best viewing practices and share live commentary on the science and mythology surrounding lunar eclipses.

Details

Venus and Jupiter Conjunction

Event Date: October 1st, 2024

See Venus and Jupiter come close in the night sky, a spectacular conjunction that promises to dazzle. Our live webinar will cover how to view this event and what makes planetary conjunctions special.

About
